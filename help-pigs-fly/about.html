Pigs in cages.

Oh, how the mighty have fallen.

For years now, pigs have been forced to roll around in their muddy cages, sullying themselves until their last day on the (literal) earth, when their carcasses are stripped bare for human consumption.

Pigs have been taunted and tormented for their entire existence. Made a mockery with idioms like, "When pigs fly," which pokes fun at pigs' inability to escape the chokehold of human society. Such idioms also present pigs as incapable of the impossible. Yet, were we not taught as youngsters to strive for the stars? Why do we lack such motivation when it comes to pigs? Humans disgrace the pig nation by referring to other humans as "Pigs" as a form of insult. Enough is enough! We wanted to change the plight of pigs all over the world. We wanted to free pigs from their oppression.

After carefully considering our options, we came to the conclusion that the only way to provide pigs with both freedom and escape from human exploits is to give pigs wings. Thus, we came up with "Pwings" - pig wings that provide flight and consequent freedom from human oppression.

Presenting Pwing!

Working together to give pigs wings.

By gifting wings onto the oppressed of the nation, we take unprecedented steps towards a future where creatures can truly carry out their pursuit- or shall we call it flight- of happiness.
